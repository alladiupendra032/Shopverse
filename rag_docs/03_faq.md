## 3. Account and Registration

### Do I need an account to place an order?
While you can browse the catalog as a guest, we require an account to place an order. Creating an account ensures your shipping details are saved, allows you to track your order history securely, and simplifies the return process if needed.

### How do I create an account?
Click on the "Login/Register" or "Profile" icon in the top navigation bar. Fill in your name, a valid email address, and create a secure password. You will receive a confirmation email to verify your address.

### I forgot my password. How do I reset it?
Go to the Login page and click on the "Forgot Password" link. Enter the email address associated with your account, and we will send you a secure link to reset your password. For security reasons, the link expires after 24 hours.

### How do I update my account information?
Once logged in, navigate to your "Profile" page. Here, you can update your name, email address, password, default shipping address, and billing information. Remember to save your changes before exiting the page.

### Is my personal information secure?
Absolutely. We take data privacy very seriously. All personal data is encrypted both in transit (using HTTPS/TLS) and at rest within our secure Supabase PostgreSQL database. Passwords are irreversibly hashed. We do not sell your data to third parties.

## 4. Shopping and Ordering

### How do I find a specific product?
You can use the search bar located in the main navigation menu. Simply type the name, category, or keywords related to the product you're looking for. Alternatively, you can browse through the specific category pages using the dropdown menus.

### The item I want is out of stock. Will it be restocked?
We regularly restock popular items. If an item is listed as "Out of Stock," you can often sign up for email notifications on the Product Detail Page to be alerted the moment it becomes available again. If the item is permanently discontinued, it will eventually be removed from the catalog.

### How do I know if an item is successfully added to my cart?
When you click "Add to Cart" on a product page, you will see a visual confirmation (usually a notification pop-up or toast message), and the cart icon in the top right corner will update its item count. You can click the cart icon at any time to review the contents.

### Can I change my order after it has been placed?
We process orders very quickly to ensure fast delivery. If you need to make changes (like updating the shipping address or canceling an item), please contact customer support immediately. If the order status has already progressed to "Shipped," we can no longer make changes, but you can initiate a return once the item arrives.

### Do you offer gift wrapping or personalized messages?
At this time, we do not offer custom gift wrapping or personalized messages as part of the standard checkout process. However, all our products are shipped in secure, unmarked packaging.

## 5. Technical Issues

### I'm experiencing an error at checkout. What should I do?
First, ensure that all mandatory fields (like shipping address and payment details) are filled correctly. If the issue persists, try clearing your browser's cache and cookies, or attempting the purchase in an "Incognito" or "Private Browsing" window. If you still encounter errors, please contact our support team with a description of the error message.

### The website is loading slowly or not displaying correctly.
Ensure you have a stable internet connection. Our platform is optimized for modern web browsers (Chrome, Firefox, Safari, Edge). If you are using an older browser version, consider updating it. Ad-blockers or aggressive privacy extensions can sometimes interfere with site functionality; try temporarily disabling them to see if it resolves the issue.

### I didn't receive an order confirmation email.
Order confirmation emails are sent automatically within minutes of a successful purchase. Please check your "Spam" or "Promotions" folders. If you still cannot find it, verify that the email address associated with your account is correct. You can always view your successful orders by logging into your account and visiting the "Order History" section in your Profile.
